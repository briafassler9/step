Pepper

On Christmas day in 2010, my parents surprised me and my sister with Pepper! She's a Yorkie-Poo, which is a mix between a yorkshire terrier and a poodle. She was named Pepper because when she was smaller, her coat was the color of pepper, so we thought it was fitting. Pepper is now 9 years old, her birthday is on October 24th where she'll hit double digits this year. She's about the size of a toddler and knows tricks like sit, down, paw, roll over, stay, walk, and more. One of her favorite things to do is sit on the couch and look out the window, barking at anything that passes. Pepper likes to play fetch and run around at the park, but sometimes she isn't too friendly with other dogs. She's an amazing dog and I love her so much.

Ginger

In October 2019, my sister rescued a dog and named her Ginger. She's 2 years old, but her birthday is unknown so instead we'll celebrate her rescue day. Also, we're not sure of her breed and the shelter didn't know, but we know she's a terrier mix of some sort. We don't know much about her past but we believe there was some type of trauma. For a couple of months, she didn't bark, and she still has bad separation anxiety. She doesn't play with any dog toys and usually spends her time sleeping. Over time, she's gotten comfier with our family and let loose. We still have a long way to go, but we show her endless support and love through her journey.
